Do you have what it takes
to be our next Front-End
Developer?

The Position

The short version is you will be assisting in the maintenance of existing projects, working closely with clients to coordinate ongoing updates to websites.

Many of Monkee-Boy’s clients require frequent adjustments and improvements to their sites to help their businesses grow on the web. For the most part, these requests come through our ticketing system. The Junior Developer will be the primary person responsible for executing these requests. This is a full time position and will be required to work from the Monkee-Boy World Headquarters in beautiful Austin, Texas.

The Requirements

This is for a junior position so expert level is not required in the list below.

  • A strong passion for learning new technologies and collaborating with a team.
  • Advanced HTML and CSS skills.
  • Ability to debug browser compatibility issues (You know about Chrome Dev Tools right?)
  • Basic knowledge of jQuery and Modernizr.
  • Basic understanding of build processes including CSS pre-processors, minification, and optimization.
  • Basic knowledge of Adobe Photoshop.
  • Fluency with the Wordpress admin area, e.g. loading and formatting content, building and editing menus, and managing categories and tags.
  • Working knowledge of WordPress code, modifying themes, custom post types, debugging plugins, and performing security checks.
  • Great organization skills with the ability to manage and bounce between multiple projects in a day.
  • Excellent communication skills in written, verbal, and documentation. You will be communicating directly with clients, most of the time through our ticketing system.

The Extras

Each of the below will get you a +1 with us. You are not required to be an expert, but bring the urge to learn.

  • Experience working with responsive design.
  • Familiar with other content management systems.
  • Experience coding email templates (responsive or not). Bonus points for knowing your way around MailChimp and Constant Contact.
  • Experience working with things like gulp, grunt, SASS, Bower, npm, Vagrant, and Capistrano.
  • Experience with git or svn. Bonus points for sending us an active GitHub profile.
  • An unnatural love of Torchy’s breakfast tacos.

The Dev Team

This application process is not a one way street. It’s important not only for you to be a good fit for us but that we are a good fit for you. We won’t take it personally if you pass on us.

  • If you’ve browsed The Troop page you might already know that the development team consists of two front end developers, one back end developer, and two developers that can’t figure out if they like front end or back end best so they just do both.
  • We love git. If you don’t, we feel pretty strongly that you’ll be convinced by your first lunch.
  • On the front end side we use SASS, gulp, jQuery, WordPress, Bower, Foundation, Susy and Vagrant.
  • On the back end our language of choice is PHP. Some of us also have experience with Ruby on Rails and Python. None of us do ASP.
  • Since we love git, you can imagine how we feel about GitHub. We actively try to contribute to open source where possible. From open sourcing some of our internal apps to submitting pull requests to projects we use. Our profile speaks for itself.
  • Outside of using WordPress we have some clients on SurrealCMS and are actively developing our own custom content management system (using CakePHP).
  • We like to dabble when we can in things like Ember.js, Angular.js, Laravel, Jekyll, and more.
  • Although the majority of the team works on Macs, you will be free to use whatever you are comfortable with.
  • We love to collaborate. Whether it’s in the office throwing around new ideas, asking each other for help, or meeting up at a nearby coffee shop for a little change of scenery.
  • Finally we are not alone in the jungle. We work intimately with an incredibly talented team.
