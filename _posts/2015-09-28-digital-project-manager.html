The Position

Monkee-Boy Web Design, Inc. is looking for a Digital Project Manager who can lead and manage multiple web design and development projects and ensure that overall project goals and objectives are met within project scope and timelines.

The Digital Project Manager will collaborate and work closely with our project team to refine existing processes as well as adapt and develop new processes as needed.

Ideal candidates will be college graduates with 4+ years of experience working in a web/creative agency with a range of clients (from small businesses to enterprise level corporations). Candidates should be very comfortable handling conflict resolution (not that it happens that often), being the single point of contact for projects, and running meetings and conference calls with clients.

Candidates should also have a thorough understanding of web browsers, web design best practices, modern web development capabilities, SEO, and digital marketing. You should not always have to "get back to clients" after checking with other team members.

Compensation

This position will be a full-time position. The candidate will be competitively compensated based on work experience, demonstration of success, and other intangibles they bring to the table. They will be able to participate in all employee incentive programs.

The Responsibilities

The ability to evaluate the breadth of client requests and their level of complexity in order to determine how they fit within project scopes will be essential to excelling in this position.

  • Take control, manage, and drive execution on all aspects of new design and development projects.
  • Become and remain the central point of contact for client communications from initial contract execution to project launch and keep issues from escalating to the executive team.
  • Understand and manage technical requirements to answer all project questions asked by internal staff and external clients.
  • Relentless communication with clients to ensure transparency — proper expectations and overall project status should always be clearly conveyed. 
  • Create and manage the master schedule of project deliverables and Monkee-Boy resources across all projects.
  • Manage and organize project materials to ensure all internal teams and clients have the necessary information to complete their tasks.
  • Understand client needs, history, and develop a close rapport to help establish mutual trust through accountability.
  • Continuously develop and evolve reports, processes, and deliverables to ensure project success.
  • Passion for learning and ability to adopt new and ever-evolving industry practices to ensure project processes stay relevant.
  • Work closely with other key team members, like the Customer Success Director, to properly plan and execute smaller projects with a limited number of staff resources that are shared across the company.

The Requirements

  • Optimistic and fun loving personality who can keep things upbeat while under pressure
  • 4+ years experience in project management and/or client services for technical web projects
  • Great people-skills, including giving respectful push-back to educate clients when necessary
  • Superhuman organizational and time management skills
  • Unprecedented attention to detail
  • Be willing to learn new skills, teach, and be mentored
  • Ability to learn through reading and online training
  • Applicant MUST live in Austin area and work out of our office
  • Most importantly, fit within the Monkee-Boy culture and love working with a diverse group of web experts with a “no ego, fun loving, happy dance, get your hands dirty to get the job done” mentality

The Extras

Each of the below will get you a +1 with us.

  • Prior experience at a web/creative agency
  • Ability to make html and css changes to a website
  • Solid understanding of WordPress CMS and related plugins
  • Experience working with B2B and B2C companies in a variety of industries
