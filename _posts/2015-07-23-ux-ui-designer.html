The Position

We’re looking for a UX/UI Designer with a sharp design eye and a passion for learning new things to join the Monkee-Boy team.

This position works closely with the senior design staff and reports to the Creative Director. A UX/UI designer is responsible for initial concepting and brainstorming, working closely with content strategy to determine features and functionality, wireframing both site and mobile projects, UI design, animation and interaction direction, and handling client maintenance requests. This is a full time position and will be required to work from the Monkee-Boy World Headquarters in beautiful Austin, Texas.

The candidate will be competitively compensated based on work experience, demonstration of success, and other intangibles they bring to the table. They will be able to participate in all employee incentive programs.

The Requirements

We need a designer who loves the web.

  • Ability to work collaboratively and take direction and feedback from Creative Director and peers
  • Excellent communication skills and a clear ability to articulate the “why” behind every design decision to other designers or clients
  • Experience working with and presenting to clients
  • A good eye for detail, color, balance, white space, and typography, and a portfolio that proves it.
  • Solid understanding of web best practices, including basic knowledge of HTML and CSS
  • A proven track record of successful Responsive Design projects
  • Understanding of web fonts, what makes a good one, and their impact on site performance
  • Understanding web accessibility best practices
  • Proficiency in Photoshop and Illustrator, preferred experience in Sketch or InVision as well
  • Ability to manage your own time and meet tight deadlines while working on multiple projects
  • 2+ years work experience designing for web / mobile, preferably in agency setting

The Extras

Each of the below will get you a +1 with us.

  • Experience designing natively for mobile platforms, iOS, Android, or other
  • Experience with custom or open source Content Management Systems
  • Active participation and contribution to the web community
  • UI Animation skills (AfterEffects, Keynote, or other)
  • UX or user research experience
  • Knowledge of print design techniques: CMYK, bleeds, color separations
  • A strong passion for learning new technologies and collaborating with a team

The Design Team

This application process is not a one way street. It’s important not only for you to be a good fit for us but that we are a good fit for you. We won’t take it personally if you pass on us.

  • If you’ve browsed The Troop page you might have seen that our design team is pretty tight-knit. We have two UX/UI Designers, a Creative Director, and you! UX/UI Designers lead their own projects and will be providing mentorship and training as well as the Creative Director.
  • We’re split between Mac and PC: those of us on Macs primarily use Sketch + Photoshop and those of us on PC use Illustrator + Photoshop. You’ll have the freedom to pick whichever setup you prefer.
  • We love InVision, and think you will too (if you don’t already!)
  • We’re big on sharing ideas and social bookmarking, whether it’s a new button animation we saw or a cool illustration on Dribbble.
  • We’re pretty active in the design community, and believe in giving back where we can. We ♥ hackathons, meetups, and panel discussions outside of work hours.
  • We love to collaborate. Whether it’s in the office throwing around new ideas, asking each other for help, or meeting up at a nearby coffee shop for a little change of scenery.
